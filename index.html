Trex Evolution!

This project demonstrates neuroevolution in the browser by training agents to become masters at the popular Google Chrome Dino Runner game. Built by members of the Diablo Valley College Computer Science Club, DVHackers.

In the simulation, trexes contain brains (neural networks) that control their decision to jump or duck when faced with obstacles. After each trex dies in a generation, they are rated on their final score and a new generation is created with higher scoring trexes being more likely to reproduce.
